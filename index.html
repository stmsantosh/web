Our Introduction

Professor, vocal coach and collaborative pianist, John Everett enjoys a very active and diverse musical career. She holds a Master of Music Degree in Collaborative Piano from the Cleveland Institute of Music, a Bachelor of Music from the University of Ottawa, and an ARCT Performer’s Diploma from the Royal Conservatory of Music.

“Music is the great uniter. An incredible force. Something that people who differ on everything and anything else can have in common.”
